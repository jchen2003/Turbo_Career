Services

With TurboCareer, users can freely navigate through our website to find internships, volunteering opportunities, mentorships, and other programs, all in New York City. For a lot of teens, transitioning from education to the real world is a rocky one. TurboCareer is here to help others realize the sheer number of opportunities around them to take that next step in life. If the user is having a hard time searching for a program, they can always use our filter tool to filter based on interest, benefits, company, location, and timing!


You can add several filters to make your search more applicable to you and your needs!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Click the get started button at the top of this website and create an account by connecting via a google account or facebook account.

What if I can't find anything in my borough or time slot?

There are a limited amount of programs avaialble; try to be more flexible by allowing more locations and times to get more results.

What if I cannot find a program that fits my needs?

We constantly check for new programs, so click the notification button to receive an email when we find a new program that fits your needs.

What if my question isn't in the faq?

Please contact us via email in the box below. Our team will get to you as soon as possible.
